POWERCORE WELDING ROD

Powercore is a flexible thermoplastic electrofusion welding rod approximately 3/16” (4.7mm) in diameter.

• Powercore uses multiple super fine electrical resistance wires inside the rod that have no effect on weld strength or longevity.

• Powercore has been extensively tested to ensure quality

• Powercore allows for weld lengths from 1 inch to 130 feet (30mm / 28m) in minutes

• Powercore comes in 1,000 and 5,000 foot spools

APPLICATIONS FOR POWERCORE

Powercore is a fully customizable plastic electrofusion welding system for a wide variety of fabrication needs

WHY USE POWERCORE?

Versatile

You can weld all very large objects with Powercore in record time, improving your efficiency.

Cost-effective

Powercore presents many opportunities for potential cost-savings by enabling assembly and welding on-site.

Easy to use

Simple 3 step process: Tac, clamp and add power. 1 - Tac Powercore rod to one of the parts to be welded. 2 - Clamp part together. 3 - Power up the rod at set power and levels and in a few minutes it's done.

POWERCORE SPECS

SHAPES

• Powercore can be used for welds of virtually any shape

• Curved or straight

• Round or square corners

• No effect on quality or strength

STRENGTH

• High quality welds from end to end

• Weld strength is the same as the plastic being welded

• Using welding rods in parallel adds strength to the surfaces being joined

• No strength loss from wires remaining in the weld
